**_Iqaa_** is a calm, beautifully designed audio platform for anyone who wants to listen to the Qur’an and nasheeds _without distractions_. There are **no ads**, **no clutter**, **and no noise**, just pure, high-quality spiritual audio in a clean, modern interface. Whether you’re studying, relaxing, praying, or unwinding after a long day, Iqaa provides a space where recitation and rhythm flow naturally. You can explore a growing library of Qur’anic tilawah, peaceful nasheeds, and curated playlists that soothe, uplift, and inspire.

### Why Iqaa?

- Zero ads — ever. Just peaceful listening.
- Minimal, beautiful design crafted for clarity and calm.
- High-quality Qur’an recitations from your favorite qaris.
- A curated collection of nasheeds with no instruments.
- Playlists for every moment — focus, reflection, worship, rest.
- Offline listening so your peace stays with you anywhere.
- Smart search & bookmarks to reach your favorite ayahs instantly.

### A calm rhythm for your day

“Iqaa” comes from the idea of rhythm — the steady, peaceful flow of recitation. The app is built to match that feeling: simple, soothing, and free from distractions.

Whether you’re memorizing, reflecting, or simply listening, Iqaa helps bring serenity back into your spiritual routine.

## Features

Iqaa is built with simplicity and peace in mind. Every feature exists to keep your listening experience clean, fast, and distraction-free.

- Ad-free Qur’an recitation from a variety of qaris
- Instrument-free nasheeds curated for calm, focus, and reflection
- Minimal UI designed for clarity and ease
- Fast, smooth playback with smart caching
- Offline listening for recitations on the go
- Bookmarks & favorites to save your moments instantly
- Search by Surah, Ayah, Qari, or Nasheed
- Dark & light themes for day and night use
  \*Cross-platform support (mobile + web planned)

## Tech Stack

Iqaa is built using lightweight, modern tools to keep the experience fast and reliable.

- Next.js / React
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Node.js for backend endpoints
- Supabase / Firebase (planned for audio storage + auth)
